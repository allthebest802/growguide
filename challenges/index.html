World Cup 2026 geography challenges — test your knowledge

The 2026 FIFA World Cup features 48 nations from every corner of the world — and each one has a capital city, a currency and a place on the map. How well do you know them? These free geography challenges test your knowledge of the World Cup nations through four different quiz formats: capital cities, currencies, population comparisons and true or false. All free, all playable on any device, no login required.

Capital city quiz — World Cup 2026

Can you name the capital cities of all 48 World Cup 2026 nations? Some are easy — Paris for France, Tokyo for Japan, London for England. Others catch people out. What's the capital of Curaçao? Where is Uzbekistan's capital? What about Cape Verde? The capital city quiz covers every World Cup nation and tests your geographical knowledge beyond the football.

Which has more? — population geography quiz

Which country has the larger population — Morocco or Portugal? Australia or Ecuador? The "Which Has More?" challenge pits countries head to head on population. It's harder than it sounds — and reveals surprising facts about the size and scale of the nations competing in the 2026 World Cup. Great for classrooms and geography enthusiasts of all ages.
